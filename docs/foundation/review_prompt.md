# Review Prompt

Perform a final technical review of the current LPIC-3 chapter.

Before making any changes, read and follow:

- docs/foundation/working-agreement.md
- the official LPI Exam 305 objectives page
- docs/foundation/review_process.md
- docs/glossary.md
- the current chapter
- the previous chapter (if applicable)

Your goals are:

1. Verify complete coverage against the official LPI Exam 305 objective.
2. Verify technical correctness using official documentation where necessary.
3. Identify and fix factual inaccuracies.
4. Identify and add missing exam-relevant topics.
5. Remove duplicate explanations.
6. Improve clarity without changing the established writing style.
7. Keep terminology consistent with previous chapters.
8. Do not introduce topics belonging to later LPIC objectives unless required for understanding and clearly marked as background.
9. Preserve the existing document structure whenever possible.
10. Produce the smallest reasonable diff.
11. Fix grammar, formatting and Markdown issues.
12. Verify commands, configuration examples and file names.
13. Verify that new exam-relevant terms are present in `docs/glossary.md`.
14. Ensure consistency with the rest of the repository.
15. Confirm the chapter Review Checklist matches the objective and project Definition of Done.
16. Confirm whether the objective still needs an exam drill before closure.

Do not rewrite the chapter unless absolutely necessary.

Act like the final technical reviewer before the chapter is committed.
